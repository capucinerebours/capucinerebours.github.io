I am committed to making research on autism accessible beyond academic 
audiences. My public engagement work spans community events, professional 
training, and media, developed in close collaboration with autistic communities 
and advocacy organizations.

## Outreach & Professional Training

**2026**
Invited panellist and facilitator for a public film discussion on autism in 
women, following the screening of *Her Difference*, as part of Brain Awareness 
Week (~150 community members).

Invited presentation at Café Autisme Toulouse on the intersection of 
transidentity and autism.

**2025**
Design and delivery of a professional training program, *Social Camouflaging: 
Consequences and Therapeutic Perspectives*, for clinicians and support staff 
within the CERESA network (Regional Centre for Education and Services for 
Autism, Midi-Pyrénées). The training examined how clinical practices may 
inadvertently reinforce camouflaging, and explored therapeutic approaches 
supporting autistic mental health and wellbeing.

**2023-2026**
Organization and facilitation of public talks on autistic burnout and barriers 
to diagnosis among autistic women and gender minorities, in collaboration with 
autism organizations and autistic community members.
